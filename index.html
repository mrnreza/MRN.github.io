About Me

This is Mohammadreza Nakhaei. I'm passionate about the intersection between artificial intelligence and robotics I'm a master's student in mechatronic engineering at the University of Tehran, Iran. This is my last semester and I'm working under the supervision of Dr. Askari, researching in solving reinforcement problems using supervised learning method, different methods of implementing artificial curiosity in RL, recurrent neural networks and application of sequential data in robotics. I finished my B.Sc. in mechanical engineering from the Iran University of Science and Technology under the supervision of Prof. Habibnejad Korayem. I was a member of the robotic research lab where I had the chance to experiment with several laboratory robots.
